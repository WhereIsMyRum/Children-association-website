How does your support help

Kids enrolled in our sponsorship program have access to the just-right mix of resources, based on location, age and life circumstances. What is sponsorship? Find out more.

Here are some of the ways your support helps to reinforce healthy habits:

1. Sharing information (presentations, pamphlets, posters and one-on-one counseling) about critical health topics tailored to children or caregivers

2. Working to identify health-related attitudes and influence them with comprehensive communications

3. Creating learning opportunities where kids can practice skills in a supportive environment

4. Fostering individual support for kids and families with health challenges

5. Using peer modeling to demonstrate healthy behavior

6. Reducing barriers to healthy behaviors.

We bring health care to Children and Families wherever they are, whatever their circumstances or income, we go where disadvantaged kids are to give them the care they won't get from anyone else. Our network of medical teams bring care to vulnerable children in disadvantaged communities.

And Here Are Ways Your Support Makes Health Services Available And Affordable:


1. Operating Medical And Dental Clinics And Pharmacies At CI Community Centers When Services Are Not Otherwise Accessible Or Affordable

2. Helping Kids And Their Families Access And Enroll In Existing Government/Public Health Insurance Programs

3. Providing Subsidies For Out-Of-Pocket Expenses Where Support Doesn't Exist

4. Creating Partnerships With Local Hospitals, Clinics And Pharmacies To Reduce Costs

5. Mapping Services To Bring Services Closer To Sponsored Communities
